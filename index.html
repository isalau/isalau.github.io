About Me

I am a 3rd year Ph.D. student in Computer Science at the University of Florida. I work under the advisement of under Dr. Juan Gilbert in the Human Experience Research Lab. In Spring of 2017, I was awarded the National Science Foundation Graduate Research Fellowship. I received my Bachelor's degree from the University of Florida in Computer Engineering with a minor in Math and a minor in Dance. My research focuses on health monitoring wearable devices, mobile development, and tech policy. In my free time, I enjoy dancing, traveling, and reading.
